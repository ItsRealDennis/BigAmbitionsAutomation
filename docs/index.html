Get started

Installed in under a minute.

A one-click installer auto-detects your game and drops everything in place.

1

Prep the game

Install MelonLoader into Big Ambitions and launch once. On Windows 11, turn off Smart App Control.

2

Run the installer

Download the release, unzip, and double-click install.bat. It finds your game and copies the mod.

3

Press F8

Launch the game and hit F8 to open BA BOT. Flip on what you want — done.

Built to fail safe

Automation you can trust to leave running.

  • ✓
    Default-OFF.

    A fresh install does nothing until you opt in, feature by feature.

  • ✓
    Reserve floor & budget.

    It never spends below the cash cushion you set — checked against the whole plan, not one buy at a time.

  • ✓
    Safety breakers.

    Low funds, unpaid rent or empty inventory halt automation and tell you why.

  • ✓
    Never writes your save.

    All settings live outside the save file. Uninstall any time and load vanilla, clean.
